Course Overview

This comprehensive beginner's yoga course is designed to introduce you to the fundamental principles and practices of yoga. Whether you're completely new to yoga or looking to strengthen your foundation, this course provides a safe and supportive environment to begin your journey.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic yoga poses (asanas) and proper alignment
  • Fundamental breathing techniques (pranayama)
  • How to create a safe home practice
  • Introduction to meditation and mindfulness
  • Understanding yoga philosophy and its benefits
  • Building strength, flexibility, and balance

Requirements

  • No prior yoga experience required
  • Yoga mat (alternatives provided if needed)
  • Comfortable clothing that allows movement
  • Open mind and willingness to learn
  • supportive environment to begin
  • introduce you to the fundamental
